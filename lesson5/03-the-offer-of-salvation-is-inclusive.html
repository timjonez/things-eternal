Bible Study Five

Eternal Salvation

Key Verse: Hebrews 5:9.

“And being made perfect [Christ’s ___ obedience to the Father’s will], he became the author of eternal ___ unto all them that obey him.”

— The Offer of Salvation Is Inclusive —

God’s gift of eternal salvation is offered to everyone; no exclusions!

  1. Salvation is for “WHOSOEVER WILL:”

    1. John 3:16 — “For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that WHOSOEVER believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life.”
    2. Revelation 22:17 — “And the Spirit and the bride say, Come. And let him that heareth say, Come. And let him that is athirst come. And WHOSOEVER WILL, let him take the water of life freely.”

  2. Salvation is for “ALL” who will come to Christ:

    1. I Timothy 2:3-4 — “For this is good and acceptable in the sight of God our Saviour; Who will have ALL men to be saved, and to come unto the knowledge of the truth.”
    2. II Peter 3:9 — “The Lord is not slack concerning his promise, as some men count slackness; but is longsuffering to us-ward, not willing that ANY should perish, but that ALL should come to repentance.”
    3. Titus 2:11 — “For the grace of God that bringeth salvation hath appeared to ALL men.” Ezekiel 18:22.
