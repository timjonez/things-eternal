Bible Study Five

Eternal Salvation

Key Verse:

Hebrews 5:9 — “And being made perfect [Christ’s perfect ___ to the Father’s will], he became the ___ of eternal salvation unto all them that obey him.”

— God Wants to Hear from You —

We have studied the Bible to learn Who God is and what God has done for you through the Lord Jesus Christ. Jesus Christ has done everything necessary to save you from your sins and give you eternal life.

The gracious invitation to be saved has been given — it is time for you to RSVP. What will your answer be? The decision is yours to make.

“That if thou [YOU] shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt ___ in thine heart that God hath ___ him from the dead, thou SHALT be saved. For with the ___ man believeth unto righteousness; and with the mouth confession is made unto salvation.” Romans 10:9-10

  1. Confess with the MOUTH — talk to God.

    1. Confess (admit) your condition —

      Luke 18:13 — “And the publican, standing afar off, would not lift up so much as his ___ unto heaven, but smote upon his ___, saying, God be merciful to me a sinner.”

      Psalm 51:3-4Psalm 51:3 — For I acknowledge my transgressions: and my sin is ever before me. Psalm 51:4 — Against thee, thee only, have I sinned, and done this evil in thy sight: that thou mightest be justified when thou speakest, and be clear when thou judgest.; Luke 15:21Luke 15:21 — And the son said unto him, Father, I have sinned against heaven, and in thy sight, and am no more worthy to be called thy son.; I Timothy 1:15I Timothy 1:15 — This is a faithful saying, and worthy of all acceptation, that Christ Jesus came into the world to save sinners; of whom I am chief..

    2. Confess (acknowledge) your need —

      John 4:15 — “The woman saith unto him, Sir, give me this water [the water of life], that I thirst not, neither come hither to draw.”

    3. Confess your total dependence upon Christ —

      John 6:68-69 — “Then Simon Peter answered him, Lord, to whom shall we go? thou hast the ___ of eternal life. And we ___ and are sure that thou art that Christ, the Son of the living God.”

      Psalm 73:25Psalm 73:25 — Whom have I in heaven but thee? and there is none upon earth that I desire beside thee.; Matthew 18:3-4Matthew 18:3 — And said, Verily I say unto you, Except ye be converted, and become as little children, ye shall not enter into the kingdom of heaven. Matthew 18:4 — Whosoever therefore shall humble himself as this little child, the same is greatest in the kingdom of heaven..

    4. ASK Christ to save you —

      Romans 10:13 — “For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved.”

  2. Believe in the HEART — not merely the head.

    1. Believe what the Bible says Who Jesus IS —

      Acts 8:37 — “And Philip said, If thou believest with all thine heart, thou mayest. And he answered and said, I believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God.”

    2. Believe what the Bible says Jesus has DONE —

      I Peter 1:21 — “Who by him do believe in God, that raised him up from the dead, and gave him glory; that your faith and hope might be in God.”
