Bible Study Five

Eternal Salvation

Key Verse: Hebrews 5:9.

“And being made perfect [Christ’s perfect obedience to the Father’s will], he became the author of eternal salvation unto all them that obey him.”

— God Wants to Hear from You —

We have studied the Bible to learn Who God is and what God has done for you through the Lord Jesus Christ. Jesus Christ has done everything necessary to save you from your sins and give you eternal life.

The gracious invitation to be saved has been given — it is time for you to RSVP. What will your answer be? The decision is yours to make.

“That if thou [YOU] shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou SHALT be saved. For with the heart man believeth unto righteousness; and with the mouth confession is made unto salvation.” Romans 10:9-10

  1. Confess with the MOUTH — talk to God.

    1. Confess (admit) your condition — Luke 18:13.

      “And the publican, standing afar off, would not lift up so much as his eyes unto heaven, but smote upon his breast, saying, God be merciful to me a sinner.”

      Psalm 51:3-4; Luke 15:21; I Timothy 1:15.

    2. Confess (acknowledge) your need — John 4:15. “The woman saith unto him, Sir, give me this water [the water of life], that I thirst not, neither come hither to draw.”
    3. Confess your total dependence upon Christ — John 6:68-69.

      “Then Simon Peter answered him, Lord, to whom shall we go? thou hast the words of eternal life. And we believe and are sure that thou art that Christ, the Son of the living God.”

      Psalm 73:25; Matthew 18:3-4.

    4. ASK Christ to save you — Romans 10:13. “For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved.”

  2. Believe in the HEART — not merely the head.

    1. Believe what the Bible says Who Jesus IS — Acts 8:37. “And Philip said, If thou believest with all thine heart, thou mayest. And he answered and said, I believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God.”
    2. Believe what the Bible says Jesus has DONE — I Peter 1:21. “Who by him do believe in God, that raised him up from the dead, and gave him glory; that your faith and hope might be in God.”

STEP ASIDE AND ENCOURAGE THE SINNER TO DO BUSINESS WITH GOD.
