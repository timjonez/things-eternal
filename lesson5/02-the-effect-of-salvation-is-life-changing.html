Bible Study Five

Eternal Salvation

Key Verse: Hebrews 5:9.

“And being made perfect [Christ’s perfect obedience to the Father’s will], he became the author of eternal salvation unto all them that obey him.”

— The Effect of Salvation Is Life-Changing —

Salvation is a supernatural work of GOD, whereby the sinner is BORN AGAIN. To be “born again” means to be made spiritually alive!

Another Bible word for the new birth is “regeneration.”

  • John 3:3-8 — “Jesus answered and said unto him, Verily, verily, I say unto thee, Except a man be born again, he cannot see the kingdom of God. Nicodemus saith unto him, How can a man be born when he is old? can he enter the second time into his mother's womb, and be born? Jesus answered, Verily, verily, I say unto thee, Except a man be born of water [physical birth] AND of the Spirit [spiritual birth], he cannot enter into the kingdom of God. That which is born of the flesh is flesh; and that which is born of the Spirit is spirit. Marvel not that I said unto thee, Ye must be born again. The wind bloweth where it listeth, and thou hearest the sound thereof, but canst not tell whence it cometh, and whither it goeth: so is every one that is born of the Spirit.”
  • Ephesians 2:1,5 — “And you hath he quickened [made spiritually alive], who were dead in trespasses and sins … Even when we were dead in sins, hath quickened us together with Christ, (by grace ye are saved).” John 6:63; Titus 3:5-6.

Just as we had nothing to do with our first (natural) birth, we have nothing to do with the second (spiritual birth). It is a work of God the Holy Spirit.

Through the new birth, God:

  1. Imparts a NEW NATURE — His nature.

    1. II Peter 1:3-4 — “According as his divine power hath given unto us all things that pertain unto life and godliness, through the knowledge of him that hath called us to glory and virtue: Whereby are given unto us exceeding great and precious promises: that by these ye might be partakers of the DIVINE NATURE, having escaped the corruption that is in the world through lust.”
    2. I John 3:9 — “Whosoever is born of God doth not commit sin; for HIS seed [the divine nature] remaineth in him: and he cannot sin, because he is born of God.”

  2. Imparts NEW LIFE — Colossians 3:4.

    “When Christ, WHO IS OUR LIFE, shall appear, then shall ye also appear with him in glory.”

    Galatians 2:20.

  3. Makes us a NEW CREATURE — II Corinthians 5:17.

    “Therefore if any man be in Christ, he is a new creature: old things are passed away; behold, all things are become new.”

    Galatians 6:15.

God doesn’t seek to “re-work” the old you. He will not do a makeover of your old life — it is spiritually dead, completely evil, and worthless.

  • John 3:6a — “That which is born of the flesh is FLESH...”
  • Romans 7:18 — “For I know that in me (that is, in my FLESH,) [the old, Adamic nature] dwelleth no good thing.”
