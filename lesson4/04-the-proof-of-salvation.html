Bible Study Four

The Eternal Son (Jesus Christ)

Key Verse: John 1:1-4,14.

“In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God. The same was in the beginning with God. All things were made by him; and without him was not any thing made that was made. In him was life; and the life was the light of men … And the Word was made flesh, and dwelt among us, (and we beheld his glory, the glory as of the only begotten of the Father,) full of grace and truth.”

— The Proof of Salvation —

The good news is that Christ not only died for our sins and was buried, but that he ROSE AGAIN — I Corinthians 15:3-4.

“For I delivered unto you first of all that which I also received, how that Christ died for our sins according to the scriptures; And that he was buried, and that HE ROSE AGAIN THE THIRD DAY ACCORDING TO THE SCRIPTURES.”

  1. The scriptures prophesied Christ would arise — Psalm 16:10.

    “For thou wilt not leave my soul in hell; neither wilt thou suffer thine Holy One to see corruption.”

    Job 19:25; Acts 2:25-31.

  2. The witness of His resurrection is irrefutable — I Corinthians 15:5-8.

    “And that he was seen of Cephas, then of the twelve: After that, he was seen of above five hundred brethren at once; of whom the greater part remain unto this present, but some are fallen asleep. After that, he was seen of James; then of all the apostles. And last of all he was seen of me [Paul] also, as of one born out of due time.”

    Matthew 28:1-8,16; Mark 16:1-8; Luke 24:3,13-34; John 20:1-10,16,19,26-29; John 21:5-7.

  3. Christ’s resurrection assures our salvation — Acts 17:31.

    “Because he hath appointed a day, in the which he will judge the world in righteousness by that man whom he hath ordained; whereof he hath given ASSURANCE unto all men, in that he hath raised him from the dead.”

    If Jesus Christ had not risen from the dead, He would be no different from any dead founder of any of the world’s religions.

  4. Christ’s resurrection secures our justification — Romans 4:24-25.

    “But for us also, to whom it [the righteousness of Christ] shall be imputed, if we believe on him that raised up Jesus our Lord from the dead; Who was delivered for our offences, and was raised again for our justification.”

    1. Justification is a term of judicial standing before the Law.
    2. When God sees me in Christ, it is “just-if-ied” never sinned.
  5. Christ’s resurrection brings eternal salvation — Acts 5:30-31.

    “The God of our fathers raised up Jesus, whom ye slew and hanged on a tree. Him hath God exalted with his right hand to be a Prince and a Saviour, for to give repentance to Israel, and forgiveness of sins.”

    Luke 24:46-47; Acts 13:37-39.

  6. Christ’s resurrection brings HOPE (confident expectation) — I Peter 1:3-5.

    “Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, which according to his abundant mercy hath begotten us again unto a lively [living] HOPE by the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead, To an inheritance incorruptible, and undefiled, and that fadeth not away, reserved in heaven for you, Who are kept by the power of God through faith unto salvation ready to be revealed in the last time.”

  7. Christ’s resurrection brings new LIFE:
    1. John 14:19 — “Yet a little while, and the world seeth me no more; but ye see me: BECAUSE I LIVE, YE SHALL LIVE ALSO.”
    2. Galatians 2:20 — “I am crucified with Christ: nevertheless I live; yet not I, but Christ liveth in me: and the life which I now live in the flesh I live by the faith of the Son of God, who loved me, and gave himself for me.”

  8. The risen Christ, at the present time:

    1. Stands as a MEDIATOR — I Timothy 2:5.

      “For there is one God, and one mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus.”

      This means sinners can approach a holy God through Him.

    2. Acts as an INTERCESSOR — Hebrews 7:25.

      “Wherefore he is able also to save them to the uttermost that come unto God by him, seeing he EVER LIVETH to make intercession [pray] for them.”

      Hebrews 4:14-16.

    3. Serves as an ADVOCATE — I John 2:1.

      “My little children, these things write I unto you, that ye sin not. And if any man sin, we have an advocate with the Father, Jesus Christ the righteous.”

  9. The power of Christ’s resurrection brings us VICTORY over sin — Romans 6:7-11.

    “For he that is dead is freed from sin. Now if we be dead with Christ, we believe that we shall also live with him: Knowing that Christ being raised from the dead dieth no more; death hath no more dominion over him. For in that he died, he died unto sin once: but in that he liveth, he liveth unto God. Likewise reckon ye also yourselves to be dead indeed unto sin, but alive unto God through Jesus Christ our Lord.”

    I Corinthians 15:57; Philippians 3:10.

  10. Because Christ lives, He is COMING AGAIN — John 14:2-3.

    “In my Father's house are many mansions: if it were not so, I would have told you. I go to prepare a place for you. And if I go and prepare a place for you, I will come again, and receive you unto myself; that where I am, there ye may be also.”

    1. Philippians 3:20-21 — “For our conversation is in heaven; from whence also we look for the Saviour, the Lord Jesus Christ: Who shall change our vile body, that it may be fashioned like unto his glorious body, according to the working whereby he is able even to subdue all things unto himself.”
    2. I John 3:2 — “Beloved, now are we the sons of God, and it doth not yet appear what we shall be: but we know that, when he shall appear, we shall be like him; for we shall see him as he is.”

It is not enough that the eternal Son of God, Jesus Christ, died and rose again. His death and resurrection must be personally appropriated if we want to be saved. Christ died for the sins of the world (for every single human being) — but He is only the Saviour of those who believe.

“For therefore we both labour and suffer reproach, because we trust in the living God, who is the Saviour of ALL men, SPECIALLY of those that believe” — I Thessalonians 4:10.

The fifth and final Bible study will teach how to actually RECEIVE the gift of eternal life.
