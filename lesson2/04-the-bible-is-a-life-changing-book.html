Bible Study Two

The Eternal Scriptures (The Bible)

Key Verse:

Psalm 119:160 — “Thy ___ is true from the beginning: and every one of thy righteous judgments endureth for ___.”

— The Bible Is a Life-Changing Book —

  1. ONLY the BIBLE gives answers to life’s most important questions:

    1. Who am I?
    2. Where did I come from?
    3. What is my purpose?
    4. Where will I go after I die?

  2. ONLY the BIBLE shows us the WAY of SALVATION:

    1. Ephesians 2:8 — “For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God.”

    2. Romans 10:17 — “So then faith cometh by hearing, and hearing by the word of God.”

    3. I Corinthians 2:5 — “That your faith should not stand in the wisdom of men, but in the power of God.”

    4. John 20:31 — “But these are written, that ye might believe that Jesus is the Christ, the Son of God; and that believing ye might have life through his name.”

    5. John 5:24 — “Verily, verily, I say unto you, He that heareth my word, and believeth on him that sent me, hath everlasting life, and shall not come into condemnation; but is passed from death unto life.”

    6. John 6:63c-e — “…the words that I [Jesus] speak unto you, they are spirit, and they are life.”
