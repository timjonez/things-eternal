“Things Eternal”

“Things Eternal” is a five-part Bible study series covering topics that really matter; that do make a difference. Why are they so important? Because they deal with eternal (forever) issues!

Our life here on planet earth is finite; it will end at some point. Therefore, the most important question is, “Where will you spend eternity?”
