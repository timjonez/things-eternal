Auxiliary Bible Study

For Those Who Are Not Sure God Exists

— Introduction —

It is honest and important to ask questions. The Bible itself invites investigation.

“Come now, and let us reason together, saith the LORD” — Isaiah 1:18.

Two Primary Witnesses

  1. Creation — The universe displays design and purpose.

    “The heavens declare the glory of God; and the firmament sheweth his handywork. Day unto day uttereth speech, and night unto night sheweth knowledge. There is no speech nor language, where their voice is not heard” — Psalm 19:1-3.

  2. Conscience — Every person has an inner sense of right and wrong.

    “Which shew the work of the law written in their hearts, their conscience also bearing witness, and their thoughts the mean while accusing or else excusing one another" — Romans 2:15.

— Invitation —

If you are willing to consider the evidence, we invite you to continue with Lesson 1 of the “Things Eternal” series. The study begins with who God is and proceeds from there.
