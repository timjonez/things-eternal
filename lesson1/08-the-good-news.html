Bible Study One

The Eternal Supreme Being (God)

Key Verse:

Deuteronomy 33:27ab — “The eternal God is thy refuge, and ___ are the everlasting arms…”

— The Good News —

This infinite, eternal, Supreme Being — Who created man — desires to have a personal relationship with you.

  1. The Lord God walked and talked with Adam and Eve in the Garden of Eden —

    Genesis 3:8-9 — “And they heard the voice of the ___ God walking in the garden in the cool of the day … And the LORD God called unto ___, and said unto him, Where art thou?”

  2. The Lord seeks to draw us unto Himself —

    Jeremiah 31:3 — “The LORD hath appeared of old unto me, saying, Yea, I have loved thee with an everlasting love: therefore with lovingkindness have I drawn thee.”

    Hosea 11:4abHosea 11:4 — I drew them with cords of a man, with bands of love: and I was to them as they that take off the yoke on their jaws, and I laid meat unto them..
  3. God desires to personally bless us forever in an eternal Heaven —

    Ephesians 2:7 — “That in the ages to come he might shew the exceeding riches of his grace in his kindness toward us through Christ Jesus.”
