Bible Study One

The Eternal Supreme Being (God)

Key Verse: Deuteronomy 33:27ab

“The eternal God is thy ___, and underneath are the everlasting arms…”

— What Is the Problem? —

  1. In the PAST — Genesis 3:23-24.

    “Therefore [because ___ sinned] the LORD God sent him [Adam] forth from the garden of Eden, to till the ground from whence he was taken. So he ___ out the man; and he placed at the east of the garden of Eden Cherubims, and a flaming sword which turned every way, to ___ the way of the tree of life.”

  2. In the PRESENT — Isaiah 59:1-2.

    “___, the LORD'S hand is not shortened, that it cannot save; neither his ear heavy, that it cannot hear: But your iniquities [sins] have ___ between you and your God, and ___ sins have hid his face from you, that he will not hear.”

  3. In the FUTURE — Revelation 21:27.

    “And there shall in no ___ enter into it [the Heavenly City] any thing that ___, neither whatsoever worketh abomination, or maketh a lie: but they which are written in the Lamb's book of ___.”

Future Bible studies will teach you how God solved this great problem once and for all, and show you the only way through which we can have a personal relationship with our wonderful Creator — in this life and in the life to come.
