Bible Study Three

The Everlasting Soul (Man)

Key Verse: Genesis 2:7.

“And the LORD God formed man of the dust of the ground, and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life; and man became a LIVING SOUL.”

— The Creation of Man —

From the first two chapters of the book of Genesis we learn that the creation of man was:

  1. DELIBERATE — Genesis 1:26.

    “And God said, Let us make man [a deliberative choice] in our image, after our likeness: and let them have dominion over the fish of the sea, and over the fowl of the air, and over the cattle, and over all the earth, and over every creeping thing that creepeth upon the earth.”

    The very fact that God deliberately chose to create man means that we are special to Him. We are “wanted.”

  2. DISTINCT from the animal kingdom — Genesis 1:20-25.

    “And God said, Let the waters bring forth abundantly the moving creature that hath life, and fowl that may fly above the earth in the open firmament of heaven. And God created great whales, and every living creature that moveth, which the waters brought forth abundantly, after their kind, and every winged fowl after his kind: and God saw that it was good. And God blessed them, saying, Be fruitful, and multiply, and fill the waters in the seas, and let fowl multiply in the earth. And the evening and the morning were the fifth day. And God said, Let the earth bring forth the living creature after his kind, cattle, and creeping thing, and beast of the earth after his kind: and it was so. And God made the beast of the earth after his kind, and cattle after their kind, and every thing that creepeth upon the earth after his kind: and God saw that it was good.”

    1. Man’s creation was a separate event. (He was not included in the creation of aquatic, atmospheric, or terrestrial animal life.)
    2. Vegetation and animals can only reproduce after their “kind.”
      • They do not evolve from one species into another.

  3. DIFFERENT from animals:

    Man was created in the image and likeness of God — Genesis 1:27.

    “So God created man in his own image, in the image of God created he him; male and female created he them.”

    1. The “image of God” means man has THREE parts:

      1. The Godhead is plural — Genesis 1:26a-c. “And God said, Let US make man in OUR image, after our likeness…”
      2. One God, three Persons — I John 5:7. “For there are three that bear record in heaven, the Father, the Word, and the Holy Ghost: and these three are one.”
      3. Man is body, soul, and spirit — I Thessalonians 5:23.

        “And the very God of peace sanctify you wholly; and I pray God your whole spirit and soul and body be preserved blameless unto the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ.”

        • The body — sense-consciousness.
        • The soul — self-consciousness.
        • The spirit — God-consciousness.
        • Your soul and spirit are the “real you” — housed in a body of clay.

    2. The “likeness of God” means man has God’s CHARACTER:

      1. Creativity — the ability to make.
      2. Personality — the ability to relate.
      3. Intellect — the ability to think.
      4. Emotion — the ability to feel.
      5. Volition — the ability to choose.
    3. Animals were not created in God’s image or likeness. Anything they do that is humanlike is instinctive.

  4. DIRECT — Genesis 2:7.

    “And the LORD God formed man of the dust of the ground, and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life; and man became a living soul.”

    1. God spoke all plant and animal life into existence en masse.
    2. Man was “handcrafted” and “one off.”
    3. Man’s SPIRIT was given directly by the LORD — Job 33:4.

      “The Spirit of God hath made me, and the breath of the Almighty hath given me life.”

    4. Human life comes directly from God. Therefore:
      1. Human life is SACRED.
      2. Human life is EVERLASTING — it does not end.

    This is the answer to the first of life’s greatest questions: “Where did I come from?”
