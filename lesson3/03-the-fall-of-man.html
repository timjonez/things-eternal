Bible Study Three

The Everlasting Soul (Man)

Key Verse: Genesis 2:7.

“And the LORD God formed man of the dust of the ground, and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life; and man became a LIVING SOUL.”

— The Fall of Man —

Genesis chapter 3 describes man’s fall into sin and its aftermath:

  1. Satan worked through the serpent to beguile Eve — Genesis 3:1.

    “Now the serpent was more subtil than any beast of the field which the LORD God had made. And he said unto the woman, Yea, hath God said, Ye shall not eat of every tree of the garden?”

    II Corinthians 11:3.

  2. Eve was deceived; Adam disobeyed willfully — I Timothy 2:14. “And Adam was not deceived, but the woman being deceived was in the transgression.”
  3. Sin brought a CURSE upon man — Genesis 3:16-17.

    “Unto the woman he said, I will greatly multiply thy sorrow and thy conception; in sorrow thou shalt bring forth children; and thy desire shall be to thy husband, and he shall rule over thee. And unto Adam he said, Because thou hast hearkened unto the voice of thy wife, and hast eaten of the tree, of which I commanded thee, saying, Thou shalt not eat of it: CURSED is the ground for thy sake; in sorrow shalt thou eat of it all the days of thy life.”

  4. Sin brought a curse upon all creation — Genesis 3:17-18.

    “And unto Adam he said, Because thou hast hearkened unto the voice of thy wife, and hast eaten of the tree, of which I commanded thee, saying, Thou shalt not eat of it: cursed is the ground for thy sake; in sorrow shalt thou eat of it all the days of thy life; Thorns also and thistles shall it bring forth to thee; and thou shalt eat the herb of the field.”

    Romans 8:22.

  5. The penalty for sin is DEATH — Genesis 2:17.

    “But of the tree of the knowledge of good and evil, thou shalt not eat of it: for in the day that thou eatest thereof thou shalt surely DIE.”

    The word “death” means a separation (never annihilation). There are three kinds of death taught in the scriptures:

    1. PHYSICAL death:

      This is the separation of man’s soul and spirit from his body. It is the form of death we can observe.

      1. Genesis 3:19.

        “In the sweat of thy face shalt thou eat bread, till thou return unto the ground; for out of it wast thou taken: for dust thou art, and unto dust shalt thou return.”

      2. Genesis 5:5. “And all the days that Adam lived were nine hundred and thirty years: AND HE DIED.”
      3. Ecclesiastes 12:7.

        “Then shall the dust return to the earth as it was: and the spirit shall return unto God who gave it.”

      Genesis 35:18; John 19:30; II Corinthians 5:1.

      Because of Adam’s sin, the day will come when loved ones and friends will view your lifeless body laid out in a coffin.

    2. SPIRITUAL death:

      This is the separation of man’s soul and spirit (the “real you”) from God. Sin separates us from God while we are living.

      1. Sin resulted in an immediate separation (estrangement) of Adam and Eve from the Lord God — Genesis 3:8.

        “And they heard the voice of the LORD God walking in the garden in the cool of the day: and Adam and his wife hid themselves from the presence of the LORD God amongst the trees of the garden.”

      2. Isaiah 59:1-2.

        “Behold, the LORD’S hand is not shortened, that it cannot save; neither his ear heavy, that it cannot hear: But your iniquities have separated between you and your God, and your sins have hid his face from you, that he will not hear.”

      3. Ephesians 2:1. “And you hath he quickened [made alive], who were dead in trespasses and sins.”

    3. ETERNAL death:

      This is the separation of a man from his Creator FOREVER in the Lake of Fire.

      1. Matthew 25:41,46.

        “Then shall he say also unto them on the left hand, Depart from me, ye cursed, into everlasting fire, prepared for the devil and his angels … And these shall go away into everlasting punishment: but the righteous into life eternal.”

      2. Luke 16:22de-23ab. “…the rich man also died, and was buried; And in HELL he lift up his eyes, being in torments…”
      3. Revelation 20:14-15.

        “And death and hell were cast into the lake of fire. This is the second death. And whosoever was not found written in the book of life was cast into the lake of fire.”

      Revelation 19:20; Revelation 21:8; Mark 9:43-48.
