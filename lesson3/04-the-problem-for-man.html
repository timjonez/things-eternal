Bible Study Three

The Everlasting Soul (Man)

Key Verse: Genesis 2:7.

“And the ___ God formed man of the dust of the ground, and breathed into his ___ the breath of life; and man became a LIVING SOUL.”

— The Problem for Man —

  1. Adam’s sin nature was passed on to ALL men — Romans 5:12.

    “___, as by one man [Adam] sin entered into the world, and ___ by sin; and so death passed upon ALL men, for that all have sinned.”

    1. Adam and Eve’s first child committed murder — Genesis 4:8.

      “And ___ talked with Abel his brother: and it came to pass, when they were in the ___, that Cain rose up against Abel his brother, and slew him.”

    2. By the time of Noah, man’s wickedness was pervasive — Genesis 6:5.

      “And GOD saw that the ___ of man was great in the earth, and that every ___ of the thoughts of his heart was only evil continually.”

    3. All men are sinners by virtue of their connection to Adam:

      1. Romans 3:10. “As it is written, There is none righteous, no, not one.”
      2. Romans 3:23. “For all have sinned, and come short of the glory of God.” Ecclesiastes 7:20; Romans 3:12.
    4. Even babies sin — Psalm 58:3. “The wicked are estranged from the womb: they go astray as soon as they be born, speaking lies.”

    5. We are all sinners:

      1. By birth — Psalm 51:5. “Behold, I was shapen in iniquity; and in sin did my mother conceive me.”
      2. By nature — Jeremiah 17:9. “The heart is deceitful above all things, and desperately wicked: who can know it?”
      3. By choice — John 3:19. “And this is the condemnation, that light is come into the world, and men loved darkness rather than light, because their deeds were evil.”
      4. By deed — Romans 1:32. “Who knowing the judgment of God, that they which commit such things are worthy of death, not only do the same, but have pleasure in them that do them.”

  2. Sin has consequences — Romans 6:23a. “For the wages of sin is DEATH...”

    This means:

    1. Spiritual death (now) — no relationship or fellowship with God; no spiritual life — Ephesians 4:18.

      “___ the understanding darkened, being alienated from the ___ of God through the ignorance that is in them, because of the blindness of their heart.”

    2. Physical death (soon) — Hebrews 9:27. “And as it is appointed unto men once to die, but after this the judgment.”
    3. The Second death (eternity) — Revelation 21:27.

      “And there shall in no ___ enter into it [Heaven] any thing that ___, neither whatsoever worketh abomination, or maketh a lie: but they which are written in the Lamb's book of ___.”

      Revelation 20:14.

  3. We are all without excuse:

    1. God codified and defined sin in His holy LAW — Romans 7:7.

      “What shall we say then? Is the law sin? God forbid. Nay, I had not ___ sin, but by the law: for I had not known lust, except the law had said, Thou shalt not covet.”

    2. Nine of the “Ten Commandments” encapsulate the moral laws of God.

      — At this point, use the “God’s Standard” study —

    3. The Law is the standard or measure by which we are judged. This standard is actually God Himself! — Matthew 5:48. “Be ye therefore perfect, even as your Father which is in heaven is perfect.”
    4. Jesus gave us a summation of the Law — Matthew 22:37-40.

      “___ said unto him, Thou shalt love the Lord thy God with ALL thy heart, and with ALL thy soul, and with ALL thy ___. This is the first and great commandment. And the second is like unto it, Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself. On these two ___ hang all the law and the prophets.”

    5. Have you obeyed this perfectly throughout your whole life? Yes? No?
    6. ONE violation of God’s Law makes us “guilty?” — James 2:10.

      “For ___ shall keep the whole law, and yet ___ in one point, he is guilty of all.”

    7. We are, in fact, totally DEFILED by sin:

      1. Mark 7:21-23 — “For from within, out of the heart of men, proceed evil thoughts, adulteries, fornications, murders, Thefts, covetousness, wickedness, deceit, lasciviousness, an evil eye, blasphemy, pride, foolishness: All these evil things come from within, and defile the man.”
      2. Revelation 21:27 — “And there shall in no wise enter into it [Heaven] any thing that defileth, neither whatsoever worketh abomination, or maketh a lie: but they which are written in the Lamb's book of life.”

      — At this point, use the “40 Sins” study —

    8. All sin is first and foremost against GOD — Psalm 51:3-4.

      “For I ___ my transgressions: and my sin is ever before me. Against thee, thee only, have I sinned, and done this evil in thy ___: that thou mightest be justified when thou speakest, and be clear when thou judgest.”

      David’s sin here was the one he committed with Bathsheba.

  4. We are all under condemnation — Romans 5:18a. “Therefore as by the offence of one [Adam] judgment came upon all men to condemnation…” John 3:18-19.

    1. God, not man, is the Judge — Romans 2:1-3.

      “___ thou art inexcusable, O man, whosoever thou art that judgest: for wherein thou judgest another, thou condemnest ___; for thou that judgest doest the same things. But we are sure that the judgment of God is according to truth against them which commit such ___. And thinkest thou this, O man, that judgest them which do such things, and doest the same, that thou shalt escape the judgment of God?”

    2. The VERDICT can only be “Guilty as charged!” — Romans 3:19.

      “Now we ___ that what things soever the law saith, it saith to them who are under the law: that ___ mouth may be stopped, and ALL the world may become guilty before God.”
